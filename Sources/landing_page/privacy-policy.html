Introduction

Pets Therapy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our mobile and desktop application Pets Therapy (the "App") available on iOS and macOS platforms.

Pets Therapy is a relaxation and wellness game designed to help users unwind and de-stress through interactive digital pet companions.

Information We Do Not Collect

Currently, Pets Therapy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about our users.

This includes:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers that can be linked to you personally
  • Location data
  • Usage patterns that can be linked to individual users
  • In-app behavior tied to personal identity
  • Contact information
  • Photos, videos, or other media
  • Financial information

Information We May Collect in the Future

To improve our app and user experience, we may implement basic analytics in future versions. If we do so:

Anonymous Usage Data Only

We may collect general, non-personal usage statistics such as:

  • App launch frequency
  • Feature usage (which pet types are most popular)
  • Session duration (how long the app is used)
  • Technical performance data (crashes, load times)
  • Digital pet interaction patterns
  • General device information (iOS version, device model - but not linked to you personally)

Key Principles

  • No Personal Identification: Any future data collection will be completely anonymous and cannot be linked back to individual users
  • Aggregated Data Only: Information will only be used in aggregate form to understand general usage patterns

Third-Party Services

Currently, Pets Therapy does not integrate with any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection platforms.

If we integrate third-party services in the future, we will update this policy and ensure any services used comply with our commitment to user privacy.

Data Storage and Security

Since we do not currently collect user data, there is no personal information stored on our servers or transmitted from your device.

All app data (such as pet preferences, settings, or custom pets) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Pets Therapy is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Your Rights and Choices

Since we do not collect personal data:

  • There is no personal data to access, modify, or delete
  • You maintain complete control over any locally stored app data
  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all local data

If we implement analytics in the future, we will provide clear opt-out mechanisms and update this policy accordingly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, especially regarding data collection practices, we will:

Your continued use of Pets Therapy after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
